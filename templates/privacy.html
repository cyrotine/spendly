Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you create an account and use Spendly, we collect the following information:

  • Account details — your name and email address provided at registration.
  • Expense data — amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions you enter.
  • Usage data — pages visited and actions taken within the app, used to improve the service.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide and improve the Spendly service:

  • To display your expense history and spending summaries.
  • To authenticate your account and keep it secure.
  • To identify and fix bugs or performance issues.

We do not use your financial data for advertising or share it with marketing platforms.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on our servers. We apply industry-standard measures including encrypted connections (HTTPS) and hashed passwords. We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time.

4. Third-Party Services

Spendly does not sell your data to third parties. We may use limited third-party services for infrastructure (e.g., hosting) that process data on our behalf under strict confidentiality agreements. No financial data is shared with external analytics or advertising services.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time. You may also export your expense records from within the app. To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the information below.

6. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please reach out through the contact information provided on the site. We aim to respond to all privacy-related inquiries within 5 business days.
